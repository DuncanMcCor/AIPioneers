The Pioneers of Artificial Intelligence

An AI Pioneer is more than just an inventor; they are a visionary who laid the foundational bricks of a new field. They are the thinkers, mathematicians, and engineers who dared to imagine machines that could reason, learn, and create. Their work, from ancient algorithms to modern deep learning architectures, has shaped the technological landscape we know today and continues to inspire the next generation of innovators. This project explores the journey of these remarkable individuals.
